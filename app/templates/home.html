Info

TrueSkill (TS)

The Trueskill value represents the lower bound of someones estimated skill μ, and is currently calulated as (μ - 2σ), where σ is the players uncertainty.

Mu (μ)

Represents the estimated skill of a player. When a player wins a game, μ increases, and when they lose, μ decreases. The magnitude of this change depends on the expected outcome and the uncertainty σ.

Sigma (σ)

Denotes the uncertainty in a player's skill estimation. A new player will have a high σ because the system is unsure of their skill. As a player plays more games, σ decreases, signifying increased confidence in the μ estimation.

Adjusted Ratings

Players are initialized based on their average pick in the bullybot, so that new players are not ovverated. Late pick players that have few games, also have a scaling penalty to their rating, as otherwise the system often overestimates thier initial skill.

Win Chance

This is derived by from the Mu and Sigma values of players in each team.
