Info

TrueSkill (TS)

The Trueskill value represents the lower bound of someone's estimated skill μ, and is currently calculated as (μ - 2σ), where σ is the player's uncertainty.

Mu (μ)

Represents the estimated skill of a player. When a player wins a game, μ increases, and when they lose μ decreases. The magnitude of this change depends on the expected outcome and the uncertainty σ.

Sigma (σ)

Denotes the uncertainty in a player's skill estimation. A new player will have a high σ because the system is unsure of their skill. With more games, σ decreases, signifying increased confidence in the μ estimation.

Adjusted Ratings

Players are initialized based on their average pick in Bullybot so that new players are not overrated. Late-pick players that have few games, also have a scaling penalty to their rating, as otherwise the system often overestimates their initial skill.

Win Chance

This is derived from the Mu and Sigma values of players in each team.
